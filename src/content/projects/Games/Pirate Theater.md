## Development Details

I tried to recreate the **Paper Theater** mini-game from **Genshin Impact** in Unity by rebuilding its panel-swapping puzzle mechanics. Using free assets from itch.io, I implemented character movement, draggable stage panels, and environmental obstacles such as bomb, falling floors, and locked sections, etc to recreate the original gameplay experience.

## My Tasks

- Designed and programmed the core gameplay mechanics in Unity using C#.
- Created a drag-and-drop panel swapping system.
- Implemented character navigation and movement control.
- Developed interactive puzzle elements including:
  * Swappable and locked panels
  * Falling floor
  * Vertical Movement
  * Hazards
  * Goal and level completion conditions
- Integrated visual assets, animations, and UI elements.

## Result

- Successfully recreated the core gameplay experience of the original Paper Theater mini-game.
- Delivered a fully playable puzzle prototype with multiple interactive mechanics and hazards.
- Demonstrated proficiency in Unity development, gameplay programming, drag-and-drop systems, and puzzle game design.
- Created an engaging level-based experience that challenges players to solve environmental puzzles by strategically rearranging stage panels.
