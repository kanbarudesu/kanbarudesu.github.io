### Overview
Trigo no Bouken is an educational game to understand trigonometry that is devoted to special angles of trigonometry such as sin, cos, tan, cosec, secan, cotan. This game is used as an alternative medium for studying mathematics in trigonometry material.

This game is made to complete my thesis.
