## Overview
A crystal VFX I created in Unity with three variations: Ice, Fire, and Emerald. I modeled the crystal mesh in Blender and created a custom Shader Graph shader to achieve the crystal look. In Unity, I used the Particle System to handle the spawn and despawn animations, and added variation-specific particle effects to give each crystal type its own visual identity.
