## Overview
A laser beam VFX I created using Unity’s Particle System as a timing exercise for VFX. I focused on building a clear charge-up, impact, and release sequence, where the impact happens just before the beam fires. I also created two variants of the beam, each with slight supporting particle adjustments to match their look and feel.
