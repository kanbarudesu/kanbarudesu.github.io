## Overview
A stylized portal VFX I created, inspired by Zenless Zone Zero, designed to look like a “void-like” tear in space.

I set up a render texture workflow where particle emitters are captured over a dark background, then I created a custom metaball shader in Unity to generate the portal shape, matching the in-game VFX. This approach allows the portal shape to feel fluid and unstable. I also made several additional particles to support the main portal VFX.
